# OMNIA v0 public demo

## One visible result

In a 10-template GSM-style mini-set:

- clause_augmented is the least stable variant in 10 out of 10 templates

## Why this matters

All three variants are often still correct.
But OMNIA detects that adding an extra clause consistently makes the output structurally more fragile.
